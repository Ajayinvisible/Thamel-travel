Everest-Base-Camp-Private-Trek

  • Overview
  • Itinerary & Map
  • Cost Details
  • Key Info

Everest Base Camp (EBC NEPAL) is the most famous trekking destination in the world and most trekked trekking trail in Nepal. Every year more trekkers are reaching the base camp. Now Everest Base Camp Trek is an adventure of 12 days trekking in the Himalayas. For this trek, you will need 15days in total including arrival and departure to/from Kathmandu. EBC trek starts with a 35 min flight from Kathmandu to Lukla, the trek starting point.


The Everest Trek leads you through one of the most beautiful trails of trekking in Nepal. It passes through Ethnic Sherpa villages, pine forests and along the Himalayas. After a 12 days trekking you will have to fly back from Lukla to Kathmandu and the trek ends here. We have customized our itinerary considering the need of acclimatization at two places; Namche and Dingboche while ascending to Everest Base Camp. Our friendly guides and helpful porters will safely lead you to the EBC and back to Kathmandu.

Highlights

  • Everest Base Camp ( 5,364 m)
  • Lukla Airport
  • Namchhe Bazar
  • Tengboche Monastery

Detail Itinerary

Day 1:

Meet and Greet at the airport and transfer to Hotel. Overnight in Kathmandu. Meals: ---
We meet you at the Tribhuwan International Airport, Kathmandu, with your name on paper. After a short brief, private car transfer to Hotel, about 20min drive.

Day 2:

Morning Breakfast, Trek preparation Day. Kathmandu Sightseeing. Overnight at same Hotel. Meals: B.
Morning Breakfast at Hotel, We will introduce you with your trek guide. Guide will check your trek gears and help you buy or rent required trek gears. In late afternoon, Guided sightseeing of Monkey Temple, the Swayambhunath Buddhist Temple. Monkey temple also offers views of Kathmandu valley. Overnight at Same hotel.

Day 3:

Kathmandu to Lukla (35-min flight; 2,800m) - Trek to Phakding (3-4 hrs; 2,652m). Meals: B,L,D.
Morning Breakfast at Hotel. At 6am, drive to domestic Airport for Lukla flights. Your guide will accompany you from right from the hotel. Upon arriving Lukla, 40min flights, refresh yourself with tea or coffee and trek to Phakding, 4 hours trek. Overnight at tea house. Included

Day 4:

Trek Phakding to Namche Bazar (3440m/11,283ft)/, 6 hours walk. Overnight at local lodge. Meals: B,L,D.
Morning Breakfast and trek to Namche Bazaar. Today, you will have the first glimpse of Mt Everest from below Namche Bazaar. the second half of the trail is straight uphill trek. Breakfast, lunch, dinner and tea house accommodation included.

Day 5:

Namche Bazar Rest Day. Day excursion to Syangboche and Everest View Hotel. Overnight at same Lodge. Meals: B.L.D.
Namche Rest Day. After breakfast, guided walk to Syangboche and Everest view Hotel. Overnight at same lodge. Breakfast, lunch, dinner and tea house accommodation included.

Day 6:

Trek Namche to Tengboche (3,867m/12684ft); 5-6 hour. Overnight at Tea House at Tengboche village. Meals: B,L,D.
Morning Breakfast at Namche and Trek to Tengboche: 5-6 hour. Breakfast, lunch, dinner and tea house accommodation included.

Day 7:

Trek Tengboche to Dingboche (4,358m/14,295 ft): 5-6 hour. Overnight at Tea house at Dingboche village. Meals: B,L,D.
Morning Breakfast at the tea house at Tengboche and trek to Dingboche village; 5-6 hour trekking. Today you reach above 4000 meters on earth. Breakfast, lunch, dinner and tea house accommodation included.

Day 8:

Acclimatization day. Day excursion to Chhukung or other nearby village/hill. Overnight at Dingboche. Meals: B,L,D.
Morning Breakfast at tea house and stay active to acclimatize as you have reached above 4000 meters. Dingboche is just 2 camp below the Everest Base Camp. Stay excited! Breakfast, lunch, dinner and tea house accommodation included.

Day 9:

Trek Dingboche to Lubuche (4,928m/16164 ft) 5-6 treks. Overnight at Local lodge. Meals: B,L,D.
Morning Breakfast at lodge at Dingboche and trek to Lubuche 5-6 hour. The third quarter of the trekking trail today is straight uphill trekking and you will feel the real energy required for EBC! Breakfast, lunch, dinner and tea house accommodation included.

Day 10:

Trek Lubuche to Everest Base Camp - Gorak Shep: 5-6 hour. You are at EBC today! Meals: B,L,D.
Morning Breakfast at lodge at Lubuche and Trek to Everest Base Camp via Gorak Shep; 6-7 hours trek. Enjoy the achievement of your lifetime goal to trek to Everest Base Camp. Your guide will help you take your (group and individual) photographs. Overnight at Gorakhshep. Breakfast, lunch, dinner and tea house accommodation included.

Day 11:

Kalapatthar Excursion to see sunrise views over Mount Everest. Trek to Pangboche, 7-8 hours trek. Meals: B,L,D.
At morning 4 am, Kalapatthar excursion to see sunrise over Mount Everest. Breakfast at Gorak Shep and trek to Pangboche or Somare, total 7-8 hour trekking. Breakfast, lunch, dinner and tea house accommodation included.

Day 12:

Trek Pangboche to Namche Bazaar; 5-6 hour trek. Overnight at Namche. Meals: B,L,D.
After breakfast at Pangboche, Trek to Namche Bazaar: 5-6 hour. Breakfast, lunch, dinner and tea house accommodation included.

Day 13:

Trek Namche to Lukla; 5-6 hour trekking. Overnight at Lukla. Meals: B,L,D.
Mornig Breakfast at Namche and trek to Lukla: 5-6 hour trekking. Today you will say goodbye to your porters. Guide will accompany your to Kathmandu. Overnight at tea house in Lukla. Breakfast, lunch, dinner and tea house accommodation included.

Day 14:

Fly back from Lukla to Kathmandu, 40 min flight. Overnight at Kathmandu. Meals: B,D.
Take an early morning flight from Lukla to Kathmandu. Airport transfers are included in Kathmandu. Overnight at best 3 star hotel accommodation in Kathmandu.We will organize a farewell dinner to get your feedback and to listen your experiences.

Day 15:

Breakfast at hotel. Airport transfer 3 hours before your flight time. Tour ENDS.
Fly back Home. We drive you to the airport 3 hours before your flight time. Breakfast, and airport transfer are included.
